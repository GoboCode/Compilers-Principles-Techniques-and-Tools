### 4.5.1
***
For the grammar ```S -> 0 S 1 | 0 1``` of Exercise 4.2.2(a), indicate the handle in each of the following right-sentential forms:

a) 000111.

b) 00*S*11.

### `Answer`
a) 01

b) 0*S*1
